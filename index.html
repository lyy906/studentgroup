WHO WE ARE

The purpose of the Asian Music Association is to link people of all cultures together through the universal language of music. AMA provides individuals in the Washington University and in the larger St. Louis area with an opportunity to gather, share, and enjoy Asian music.

AMA also seeks to foster an interest and appreciation for Asian music among non-Asian populations and eventually spread Asian music to the whole Washington University community.

AMA hosts events such as karaoke nights, singing contests, and a club party, and currently features Chinese, Korean, Indian, and Japanese music.
